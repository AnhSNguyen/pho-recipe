Introduction

Pho is the Vietnamese traditional noodle soup including Chicken Soup and Beef Soup. There are many recipe to cook Pho, the taste may be varied from province to province, and from family to family. In this article I would like to introduce my Homemade Beef Pho.

Ingredients for 6 bowls of Beef Pho

  • 2lb of Chuck Beef
  • 0.6lb dry rice noodles
  • 3.8 liters of water
  • 1 big yellow onion
  • piece of ginger
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 piece of cinnamon
  • pinch of whole peppercorn
  • 1 small bunch of green onion
  • 1 small bunch of cilantro
  • pinch of salt
  • 2-3 table spoons of fish sauce
  • 2-3 table spoon of Bouillon of roasted chicken base or roasted beef base, concentrated type
  • 1 lemon
  • Sriracha hot chili sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil 2lb of chuck beef in 3.8 liters of water with l/2 yellow onion, pinch of salt, remove foam, then simmer for about 35 to 45 minutes, until chuck beef is cooked. Remove beef to plate and let it cool. Remove the cooked yellow onion
  2. In the same time boil rice noodles according to the package instructions
  3. Toast ginger and remaining 1/2 yellow onion until fragrant and golden, and crush. Quickly toast cinnamon, peppercorns and star anise. Then add all to the pot of broth.
  4. Place 2 or 3 table spoons of Bouillon, 2 or 3 table spoons of fish sauce in boiling broth pot for fine taste, simmer about 15 minutes
  5. Slice green onions, cilantro, and red onions
  6. Slice the cooled beef into thin, wide slices
  7. Place cooked rice noodles to 1/3 bowl, top with beef slices, red onions, green onions and cilantro
  8. Pour boiling broth into the Pho bowl and it is done.
  9. Add lemon and hot chili sauce as needed

Enjoy Homemade Pho
